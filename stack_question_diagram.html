The Closed-Form Formula
Catalan Number
C(n) = (2n)! ÷ ((n+1)! × n!)
For N = 3: C(3) = 6! ÷ (4! × 3!) = 720 ÷ (24 × 6) = 720 ÷ 144 = 5 ✓

⚠ Why this question is hard: Knowing the answer is "Catalan Number" is only 10% of the solution. The real challenge is proving WHY stack permutations follow the Catalan formula. The proof requires the Reflection Principle (André, 1887), bijection to Dyck Paths, and the Cycle Lemma (Dvoretzky & Motzkin, 1947) — topics not covered in any standard Data Structures textbook.
